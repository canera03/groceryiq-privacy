1. Overview

Aisle Save ("we," "us," or "our") is a free crowdsourced grocery price comparison app. We help shoppers find the cheapest prices at nearby stores by letting the community submit, confirm, and browse real-time grocery prices — like GasBuddy, but for groceries.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you use the Aisle Save mobile application, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have over your data.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties. Ever.

By creating an account or using Aisle Save, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

2. What We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address — used to log in and recover your account.
  • Display name — shown on leaderboards and your public profile.
  • Password — stored as a secure one-way hash; we never see your plaintext password.

2.2 Price Submissions

When you submit a grocery price, we collect:

  • The item name, brand, and size.
  • The price you observed.
  • The store where you saw it.
  • The date of the price (from the receipt or your device date).
  • An optional photo of the price tag or receipt.
  • Your user ID (to award points and detect abuse).

Price submissions are public by design — they are the core product. Your display name is shown alongside prices you submit.

2.3 Location Data

Aisle Save requests access to your device's GPS location to show you nearby stores and relevant prices. Location is used only in the moment you are actively using the app. We do not track your location in the background and we do not store a history of your location.

2.4 Device & Usage Data (Analytics)

We collect anonymous, non-identifying usage events to understand how people use the app and to improve it. This includes events such as:

  • Screens viewed
  • Search terms entered
  • Buttons tapped (e.g., "Submit Price," "Optimize List")
  • App version and device platform (iOS)

These events are sent to PostHog (see Section 4). We configure PostHog to not collect your name, email, or any other personally identifiable information.

2.5 Receipt Photos

If you use the receipt scanning feature, you may upload a photo of your grocery receipt. We send this image to an AI service (Claude by Anthropic) to extract item names, prices, and the receipt date. The photo is processed and then discarded — we do not store your receipt images long-term.

2.6 Points & Activity

We maintain a record of the points you have earned and the actions that generated them (e.g., price submitted, price confirmed). This is used to power the leaderboard and your profile page.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and secure your account.
  • Show you grocery prices near your current location.
  • Display crowdsourced price data submitted by you and other users.
  • Calculate your points balance and rank on the leaderboard.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive submissions (e.g., fake prices, spam accounts).
  • Process receipt photos to extract price data on your behalf.
  • Understand which app features are used most so we can improve them.
  • Send you transactional emails if needed (e.g., password reset) — we do not send marketing emails.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not build advertising profiles.

4. Third-Party Services

Aisle Save relies on a small number of trusted third-party services:

Supabase (Data Storage & Authentication)

Your account information, submitted prices, shopping lists, and points history are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted by Supabase. Supabase provides authentication (login/signup) and enforces Row Level Security so that users can only access data they are authorized to see.

Supabase Privacy Policy

PostHog (Analytics)

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics. PostHog is configured with autocapture disabled — only the specific events we explicitly define are sent. No name, email, or precise location is ever sent to PostHog.

PostHog Privacy Policy

Anthropic Claude (AI — Receipt OCR)

When you scan a receipt, the image is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to extract item names, prices, and the receipt date. The image is not stored by us after processing. Anthropic processes the image according to their data handling policies.

Anthropic Privacy Policy

Google Maps (Location & Maps)

We use Google Maps to display store locations and calculate distances. Your location is sent to Google only when you actively use map or "Near Me" features.

Google Privacy Policy

Open Food Facts (Barcode Lookup)

When you scan a product barcode, we query the Open Food Facts public database to retrieve the product name and size. Open Food Facts is a nonprofit open database; your query includes the barcode number only.

Open Food Facts Privacy Policy

Railway (Hosting)

Our backend server is hosted on Railway. Railway may log standard server request metadata (IP addresses, timestamps) for infrastructure purposes.

Railway Privacy Policy

5. Data Sharing & Selling

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party.

We share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers listed in Section 4 — only to the extent necessary to operate the app (e.g., storing your data in Supabase).
  • With other users (public data only) — your display name and the prices you submit are visible to all Aisle Save users. Your email is never shared.
  • If required by law — we may disclose information if required by a valid legal process (court order, subpoena). We will notify you if permitted to do so.
  • To protect safety — if we believe disclosure is necessary to prevent imminent harm or fraud.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account information and submitted data for as long as your account is active. Price submissions are kept to maintain the historical price database that makes Aisle Save useful to the community.

Receipt photos are not retained after OCR processing is complete.

Location data is never persisted — it is used only in-session to compute nearby stores.

When you delete your account (see Section 7), all personal information associated with your account is permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

You can view your profile information, submitted prices, and points history at any time within the app on the Profile screen.

Correction

You can update your display name through the Profile screen. To update your email address, contact us at the address in Section 10.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated personal data at any time by contacting us at groceryiq703@gmail.com. We will process the request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Note: price submissions are crowdsourced community data. After account deletion, historical price entries may be retained in anonymized form (with your display name removed) to preserve the integrity of the community price database.

Portability

You may request a copy of your personal data in a common machine-readable format by contacting us.

Opt Out of Analytics

Analytics collection is anonymous by default. If you prefer to opt out entirely, contact us and we will disable PostHog tracking for your account.

Location Permissions

You can revoke location permission at any time through your device's Settings app. The app will still function but will not be able to show nearby stores or distance-based price results.

8. Children's Privacy

Aisle Save is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account, please contact us and we will delete the account and associated data promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as the app evolves. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Show an in-app notice the next time you open Aisle Save.

Continued use of the app after the effective date of any change constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. If you disagree with the changes, you may delete your account at any time.
